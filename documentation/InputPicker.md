`InputPicker` (component)
=========================

On iOS we always want the input field to expand downwards
to reveal the picker. On Android, we want to render the
Picker in dialog form when touched.

Props
-----

### `editable`

type: `bool`
defaultValue: `true`


### `label` (required)

type: `string`


### `onToggleExpansion` (required)

type: `func`


### `value`

type: `string`
